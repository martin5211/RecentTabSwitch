Recent Tab Switch

Switch tabs in most-recently-used order with an Alt+Tab-style switcher. Hold the modifier and tap the trigger key to cycle through recent tabs; release to switch. Add Shift to cycle backwards.

Trigger shortcut

Uses Ctrl + < (the key right of left-Shift) on ISO/Spanish keyboards, and Ctrl + ` on ANSI/English.

Switcher

Applies to the shortcut bound at chrome://extensions/shortcuts. Normal pages always show the full modal.

Thumbnail blocklist

One domain per line. These pages are never screenshotted (subdomains included). Incognito tabs are always skipped.
